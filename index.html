About the Lab

Landslides are globally recognized as widespread and devastating geohazards, posing significant risks to human life and property. Susceptibility mapping plays a crucial role in pinpointing areas prone to landslides, while exposure assessment evaluates the vulnerability of communities and assets in these high-risk zones. Together, these assessments are vital for mitigating the impact of landslides. They inform proactive measures such as land use planning and infrastructure development strategies aimed at reducing risks and enhancing resilience against this natural hazard.
